Referring to people and things: nouns, pronouns, and determiners

English Chinese Grammar
I got some scissors. 我拿到了一把剪刀 表示一件衣服或一件工具，在名词前面用some
Three of the people were killed 集体名词前面不能加数词
I’m sure Mum will be pleased 家族成员的名词作为称呼要大写
The rich have benefited more than the poor 用作名词的形容词作主语，动词用复数
to help break the barriers between young and old 提到的是两类人，可以省略the
There are three hundred dead unemployed&dead前可以加数词
He is still exploring the limits of the possible 他仍在探索可能的极限 谈论的是事物的性质时，可以用the加形容词
the Irish and the Japanese 以-ch,-sh,-se,-ss结尾的国籍形容词可以在前面加the 表示一国人
